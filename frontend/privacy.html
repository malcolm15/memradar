Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

MemRadar is straightforward about data. Here's exactly what we collect, why, and what we do with it.

What we collect

  • Email addresses — only if you sign up for a price alert. We ask for nothing else.
  • Basic analytics — anonymous page view data to understand which features are being used. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

How we use your email

Solely to send the price alert you requested. Once an alert triggers, your email is no longer needed for that alert. We do not send marketing emails, we do not sell your email address, and we do not share it with third parties — including Best Buy.

Data retention

Alert email addresses are deleted after the alert is triggered or after 12 months of inactivity, whichever comes first. You can also request deletion at any time by emailing hello@memradar.com.

Affiliate links

Some product links on MemRadar are affiliate links. Clicking them and making a purchase may earn us a small commission. No data about you is shared with retailers as part of this — it's a standard referral arrangement. See our Affiliate Disclosure for more detail.

Third-party services

  • Best Buy — we fetch price and product data from the Best Buy API. Your use of MemRadar does not create any relationship between you and Best Buy.
  • Supabase — our database, hosted in the United States. Email addresses for alerts are stored here.

Cookies and analytics

MemRadar uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. This may set anonymous cookies to measure traffic and usage patterns. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hello@memradar.com.
