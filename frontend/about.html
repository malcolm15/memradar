About MemRadar

Built for PC builders who are tired of guessing when to buy.

RAM prices crashed over 60% in 2023, then bounced back hard over the following year. Most people missed both moves — not because they weren't paying attention, but because there was nowhere good to watch it happen.

MemRadar exists to fix that. It tracks prices on RAM and SSDs across major retailers, stores the history, and alerts you when something you've been watching hits your target price. No fluff, no noise — just the data you need to know whether now is a good time to buy.

This is a passion project, built by a PC builder for PC builders. It's not backed by a company, and it's not trying to be a shopping aggregator. It's a focused tool for people who already know exactly what they want and are waiting for the right moment.

MemRadar is early and actively being built. If something seems off or you have an idea, get in touch — feedback from real users shapes what gets built next.
