Everything you need to know about how MemRadar works.

MemRadar is a free RAM and SSD price tracker built for PC builders. We monitor prices across major retailers including Best Buy, Amazon, and Newegg, store historical price data, and alert you when a product drops to your target price. Our goal is to give you the intelligence to know exactly when to buy.

Prices are updated daily. We fetch current prices from our retail partners every morning and store a snapshot so you can see how prices have changed over time.

We currently track prices from Best Buy, Amazon, and Newegg. We're actively working to add more retailers over time. Note: Amazon pricing is shown for reference only and is not included in price history charts, as required by Amazon's operating agreement.

Set a target price for any product and enter your email address. When that product's price drops to or below your target, we'll send you a one-time email notification with a direct link to buy. No account required. Just your email and your target price.

Amazon's operating agreement prohibits displaying their historical pricing data in charts. We show Amazon's current price as a buying option but cannot include it in price trend charts. Best Buy and Newegg data is fully charted.

Our price history reflects actual prices fetched from retailers at the time of each daily update. Prices shown are the listed retail price and may not include temporary flash sales or prices that changed between our daily fetches.
